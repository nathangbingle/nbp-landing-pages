What Is Digital Media Day

A unique experience that creates memories and content.

Looking for a way to create memorable bonding experiences for your team while generating social media content for the season? A Digital Media Day Session is exactly what you need. We bring all the essentials — big lights, fog machines, light tubes, and any props you can imagine — to make this exciting and fun for every player.

This session is ideal for smaller groups like varsity teams, seniors, or individual teams. It is not intended to replace your team's portrait or yearbook session.
