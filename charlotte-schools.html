How It Works

Simple for your school.
Seamless for families.

01 · Before Picture Day

  • Set up date(s) and times with your account specialist
  • Send class contact data to your account specialist
  • Tell parents — we'll email them too

02 · Actual Picture Day

  • Our photographer sets up and is ready to receive students
  • Students receive individual, class, and sibling pictures
  • Each student gets a handout with their customized gallery link

03 · After Picture Day

  • Parents receive text/email when gallery is available
  • Gallery is available to purchase for 2 weeks before being archived
  • All customer service is handled between NBP and the parent
  • School chooses: ship to org or to student's home
  • Commissions and product given to your school

Benefits for Schools

  • Sales commission opportunity
  • Complimentary class photos opportunity
  • Staff discount on pictures
  • 100% customer service between parents and NBP — not your staff
